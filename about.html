Donate
Blind Low Vision NZ has decided on a challenge for everyone to participate in.
By joining in, you can help by raising awareness for people with low vision or blindness and get people to donate for our cause.

What is the Challenge?
It is very simple. You are not allowed to use your phone for 24 hours straight. You must find other ways to entertain yourself withought your phone.

Don't forget to include your friends and family to join in with you.
